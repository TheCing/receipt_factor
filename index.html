How to use

1. Take screenshots while scrolling
・Supports the Hall of Fame Uma Musume skill, inheritance, and training info tabs, Team Race score info, and the race entrant screen.
・Make sure at least two rows overlap between screenshots.
・Avoid capturing anything outside the Uma Musume window (e.g. phone notifications).
・As of Ver3.0, most scrolling screens are supported, including the support card list and race results table.
　For these screens, capture so that about 1/4 of the scrollable area overlaps between screenshots.
・As of Ver3.1, Champions Meeting "gougai" extras can be stitched.
　These are always laid out horizontally. Left-to-right order matches the order images are imported.

2. Import screenshots into this site
・Import order doesn't matter — they're sorted automatically during processing.
・Remove unwanted images by tapping the ✕ in the upper-left of each preview.
・You can import multiple characters at once.
・Paste-from-clipboard only works on supported browsers/OSes.
　Tested on Chrome and Edge on Windows 11.
　Ctrl+V also works for pasting from the clipboard.

3. Tap the Create button
・Processing takes anywhere from instant to a few dozen seconds depending on image count.
・If overlap can't be determined, images are simply stacked top-to-bottom.

4. Check the output receipt image and tap a save button
・Multiple characters are output side-by-side.
・Tap the output image to toggle between 1:1 scale and fit-to-width.
・The Reset button clears all previews and results so you can start over.
・On iOS, long-press the image to save it to your Photos app.
・Copy-to-clipboard only works on supported browsers/OSes.
　Tested on Chrome and Edge on Windows 11.

β. Add skill icons to factors
・Adds a skill icon to each factor.
・Stat-boosting factors (scenario factors, etc.) get a blue icon.
・If a factor name can't be read correctly, [?] is added.
・Accuracy drops with lower-resolution images. Images at least 1500px tall are recommended.
・Skill data provided by U-tools.
